The Lion King

More here

The Lion King" (2019) is a captivating reinvention of Disney's animated classic, offering stunning visual realism. This adaptation retains Simba's timeless story and conveys lessons about courage, responsibility and finding one's destiny. With a stellar voice cast and a memorable soundtrack, this film continues to celebrate the magic of "Hakuna Matata.

Jon Favreau

More here

Jon Favreau, visionary director of "The Lion King" (2019), has left his mark on cinema. From his humble beginnings to his reinvention of film, he is an inescapable creative genius, pushing the boundaries of filmmaking. His impressive legacy inspires anticipation for his future projects.

Renowned philosopher Marianne Chaillan demonstrates how cinema can shed light on existential questions, making philosophy accessible to a wide audience. The book and film complement each other admirably, offering a stimulating reflection on happiness, the meaning of life and philosophy.

Hakuna Matata

More here

"Hakuna Matata", the cult melody from "The Lion King", embodies a philosophy of life advocating carefree, light-hearted fun. Discover how this song transcends generations, reminding us of the importance of joy and the present moment.
