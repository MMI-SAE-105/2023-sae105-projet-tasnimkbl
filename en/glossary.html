Glossary

Hakuna Matata

A Swahili expression literally meaning "no problem" or "no worries". It became famous thanks to the Disney film "The Lion King" and is associated with a carefree philosophy of life.

Philosophy of life

An approach to life based on a set of beliefs and values that guide a person's choices and actions. The "Hakuna Matata" philosophy of life advocates lightness, joy and acceptance of what happens

Film adaptation

The process of transforming a text, story or literary work into a film. The Lion King" is a film adaptation of the famous Disney cartoon.
